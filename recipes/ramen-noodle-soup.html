Ramen Noodle Soup

This soup is just very very good....you can find ramen noodles at most supermarkets, or at Asian grocery stores.

  • 3 and a half cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (3.5 ounces) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • half teaspoon chili oil
  • half teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced
